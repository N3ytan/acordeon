FAQ

  • What are virtual reality glasses?

    VR glasses, or virtual reality glasses, are an accessory that you put on your face and attach to your head. If you've ever watched a 3D movie, you needed to wear a pair of glasses, right? So, these virtual reality ones are similar to them, but the screen you see in three dimensions is that of your smartphone

  • How do VR glasses work?

    Each of your eyes sees a different image. If in doubt, just test it. Cover one eye and watch with the other. Then switch! To see with both eyes, your brain needs to piece these images together with depth, height, and width. In the case of VR glasses, an application takes the place of our brain.

  • What models are available?

    Compatible with Android and IOS smartphones, the VR Box comes with a Bluetooth control so you can enjoy the experience of using the virtual reality glasses more. With it, you can shoot in a shooting game, use it as a sword in a medieval game and so on.
